Chosen Technology/Topic

Our team had chosen the topic of artificial organs because we are interested in how artificial organs function and we found it interesting that the advancement of such technology has allowed for the development of better artificial organs, but there are also several risks and potential negative implications on patients.

We wanted to explore these positives and negatives to see if artificial organs are safe enough for patients in their current state
