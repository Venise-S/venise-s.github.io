Technology Choices

Artificial organs still have a long way to go in terms of advancement in replicating the functions of actual organs, and if the technology will be safe for patients.

As of the present day, artificial organ transplants still pose a high risk to the patient, but making the decision to go along with the transplant or face a life threating disease depends entirely on the patient and their situation.
